# AR Feature Instructions

To test the AR feature:
1. Open the website on your phone.
2. Click the 'SCAN ME' button.
3. Grant camera permissions.
4. Point your phone at a "Hiro" marker.
   - You can find the Hiro marker here: https://ar-js-org.github.io/AR.js/data/images/HIRO.png
5. The 3D coffee model will appear live on your screen!
